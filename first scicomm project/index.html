Welcome to the AI-Driven Embryo Classification Blog

How AI is being used in developmental biology to predict embryo outcomes early in development.

Model organisms or model embryos in this instance, are animals or plants that are used as models for humans during experiments. The point of using model organisms is because animal cells are almost identical throughout species, and testing on humans is unethical. Model organisms also relatively easy to keep in lab, and are easy to reproduce.

AI is now being used to decide which embryos will be most succesfful in development. Using mouse embryos, a deep learning AI named StembryoNet has been trained with hundreds of photos of mouse embryos in different stages of development in order to predict the outcome of the embryos.

This new use of AI technology is revolutionary to medical and embryology research because it can show us which embryos will be successful in development and if not what an unhealthy embryo looks like early in development and being able to pinpoint where things started going wrong.
